Point Count Help

Version 1.0

12.06.2017

This page deals and displays (bridge) hands. The user can count hand points and compare to the computed values shown.

  • click Practice to go to another page.
  • click Deal Hand to deal a new bridge hand.
  • mouse over the table position (e.g."North") of a hand to show high card and length points for that hand.
  • click Show/Hide All Points to toggle display of high card and length points for all hands.
  • click Stats to hide/show point count probablity info.
  • click Info to show point counting rules used in this page.
  • click Help to show this message.
Problems, questions, comments: email me.

Point Count Info

High Card Point Values:

  • A : 4
  • K : 3
  • Q : 2
  • J : 1

Length Point Values: +1 for every card in suit with length greater than 4, e.g

  • 5 card suit : 1
  • 6 card suit : 2
  • etc...

Probability of Hands with Point Values

Click on Get Stats to generate point value statistics on 5000 deals (20000 hands).

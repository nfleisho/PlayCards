1 NT Response Help

Version 1.0

16.06.2017

This page deals and displays (bridge) hands with a 1 NT opening bid. The user can determine bid responses and compare to the computed values shown. It is Assumed that there is no denfensive bidding interference.

  • select from Practice to go to another page.
  • click Deal Hand to deal a new bridge hand.
  • mouse over the table position (e.g."North") of a hand to show high card and length points for that hand.
  • click Show Next Bid for a computer display of partner's bid.
  • click Stats to hide/show 1 NT bid and response probablity info.
  • click Info to show bid response rules used in this page.
  • click Help to show this message.
Problems, questions, comments: email me.

1 NT Response Info

Responder Bid

Points in Hand Hand Distribution Bid
more than 9 tot more than 5 ♠/♥ 4 ♠/♥(signoff)
more than 9 tot more than 5 ♦/♣ 3 NT(signoff)
more than 9 tot 5 ♠/♥ 3 ♠/♥(forcing)
more than 9 tot otherwise 3 NT(signoff)
8-9 tot more than 4 ♠/♥ 3 ♠/♥(forcing)
8-9 tot otherwise 2 NT(invite)
0-7 tot more than 4 suit 2 suit(signoff)
0-7 tot otherwise pass(signoff)

Declarer Re-Bid

Response Points in Hand Hand Distribution Bid
all signoff pass
3 ♠/♥ more than 2 ♠/♥ 4 ♠/♥
3 ♠/♥ less than 3 ♠/♥ 3 NT
2 NT more than 15 HCP 3 NT
2 NT 15 HCP pass

Definitions:

  • signoff - expected next bid is pass
  • forcing - expected next bid cannot be pass
  • invite - expected next bid among choices

Notes:

  • no Stayman
  • no Jacobi transfers
  • no slam bids

No Trump Distribution Probabilities

There are three possible suit distributions consistent with the "balanced" requirement for No Trump. The (calculated) probabilities of their occurrance are shown below:

  • 5-3-3-2 : 33%
  • 4-4-3-2 : 45%
  • 4-3-3-3 : 22%

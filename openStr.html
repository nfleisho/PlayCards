Opening Bid Help

Version 1.0

16.06.2017

This page deals and displays (bridge) hands. The user can determine opening bids and compare to the computed values shown.

  • select from Practice to go to another page.
  • click Deal Hand to deal a new bridge hand.
  • mouse over the table position (e.g."North") of a hand to show high card and length points for that hand.
  • click Show Opening Bid for a computer display of bids up to and including the opener.
  • click Stats to hide/show opening bid probablity info.
  • click Info to show opening bid rules used in this page.
  • click Help to show this message.
